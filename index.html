Who We Are

About Us

Lonestar Landworks & Demolition is a locally owned and operated site work company based in Kilgore, TX. We run a Kubota SVL97-2 enclosed tracked skid steer — one of the most capable machines in its class — and we own it outright. No rental markups, ever.

We serve residential customers, mobile home dealers, real estate investors, GCs, oilfield contractors, and commercial properties across East Texas. No surprise charges. No scope creep.

We're also partnered with trusted local tradesmen for septic, plumbing, electrical, tree services, and asphalt — so if your project needs more than dirt work, we can coordinate the whole thing. One point of contact, start to finish.
